^_^Title^_^NBC reups 'Last Call with Carson Daly' for 13th season ^_^Story^_^NBC has renewed the late-night on-location series "Last Call with Carson Daly" for a 13th season, according to a news release Wednesday from the network.The interview show airs at 1:35 a.m. ET.Daly also is the host of "The Voice," a singing competition show that also airs on NBC.The move comes after NBC announced a future change to its late-night lineup, bringing in Jimmy Fallon to host the "Tonight Show" in 2014.
