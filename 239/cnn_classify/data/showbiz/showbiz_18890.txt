^_^Title^_^Muppets' Jane Henson dies of cancer^_^Story^_^Jane Nebel Henson -- who was married to the late Muppets creator Jim Henson and was instrumental in the development of the world-famous puppets -- died Tuesday morning, a representative for the Jim Henson Company said. She was 79.Henson died at her home in Connecticut after a "long battle with cancer," a written statement from the company said.The two met in a puppetry class at the University of Maryland in 1954, the release said.
