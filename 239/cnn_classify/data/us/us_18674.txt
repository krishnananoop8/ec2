^_^Title^_^Man charged with making bomb threat near White House^_^Story^_^A Pennsylvania man was arrested Wednesday on suspicion of making a bomb threat near the White House, according to the Secret Service and Washington's Metropolitan Police Department.Krzysztof Wasik, 44, of Hazel Township, Pennsylvania, walked up to a White House gate Wednesday morning and told guards he had a bomb in his vehicle that was set to explode, police said.The city's bomb disposal squad searched a truck nearby, but no hazardous materials were found, police said.Wasik was charged with felony threats, police said.CNN's Paul Courson and Alex Mooney contributed to this report.
