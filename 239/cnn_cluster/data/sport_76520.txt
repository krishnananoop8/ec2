This has been a necessarily thorough process in order to understand the cause of the accident."We have now concluded our investigatory work and can again focus on the priority, which continues to be Maria's wellbeing. In that regard, we continue to support Maria and the De Villota family in any way we can."De Villota family touched by supportDe Villota, who was driving the MR01 for the first time before the crash, is one of only two female drivers contracted to an F1 team.
