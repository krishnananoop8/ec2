^_^Title^_^By the numbers: Syria deaths^_^Story^_^Nearly 70,000 people have been killed in Syria since unrest began in the country two years ago, according to the latest estimate from the United Nations.And that might actually be an underestimate. High Commissioner for Human Rights Navi Pillay said Syria's raging civil war has made it "increasingly challenging" to collect accurate and reliable data.Opposition: March was deadliest month in
