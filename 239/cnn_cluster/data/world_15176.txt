^_^Title^_^English coroner to conduct inquest into Russian tycoon's death^_^Story^_^LONDON English medical examiners will open an inquest on Thursday into the death of the expatriate Russian tycoon Boris Berezovsky, police announced Tuesday.Berezovsky, 67, was found dead on Saturday at a country estate south of London, triggering speculation about the fate of a onetime tycoon known for his opposition to Russian President Vladimir Putin. Police have said the death was consistent with hanging and that there were no signs of struggle.An inquest by a coroner is standard procedure in the case of unnatural deaths in England.
