^_^Title^_^Abbas accepts prime minister's resignation^_^Story^_^Jerusalem Palestinian President Mahmoud Abbas on Saturday accepted the resignation of Prime Minister Salam Fayyad, a Palestinian Authority spokeswoman told CNN.The official Palestinian Authority news agency Wafa published a statement from Abbas, who met with Fayyad at the presidential compound in Ramallah.The prime minister gave the president his resignation; Abbas asked Fayyad to lead a caretaker government until a new one is formed.Fayyad's resignation comes after longtime internal policy disputes between the two men.CNN's Kareem Khadder reported from Jerusalem and Amir Ahmed from Atlanta.
