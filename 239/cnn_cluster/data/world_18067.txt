^_^Title^_^Pakistani Taliban denies involvement in Boston bombings^_^Story^_^Read a version of this story on CNN Arabic.The Pakistani Taliban denied any involvement in a double bombing at the Boston Marathon that killed at least three people, spokesman Ihsanullah Ihsan said Wednesday."Wherever we find Americans we will kill them, but we don't have any connection with the Boston Explosions," Ihsan said.The Pakistani Taliban claimed responsibility for the Times Square bombing in 2010.
