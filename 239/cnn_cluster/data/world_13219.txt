^_^Title^_^In Colombia, rebels free 2 Germans^_^Story^_^Two German citizens held by the Colombian rebel group National Liberation Army since November were released Friday in a rural part of northeast Colombia, the International Committee of the Red Cross said.The civilians were handed over to a humanitarian team made up of Red Cross delegates and two members of a committee set up to facilitate dialogue with the armed group.Colombia has been seeking an end to nearly 50 years of conflict with rebels.
