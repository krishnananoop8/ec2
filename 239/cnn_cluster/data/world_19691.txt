The deployments come as U.S. and South Korean officials warn Pyongyang could be preparing for another provocative move after weeks of belligerent rhetoric.North Korea tells foreigners in South to take safety measuresMonday, Suga had said the government would not publicize any missile-defense deployment, saying "It would show our strategy to North Korea."North Korea's missile capabilitiesCNN's Yoko Wakatsuki contributed to this report.
